# Savius
*Savius* is the protagonist of the story, "Tale of Savius and Dorkel". Savius is a tall, lean, and agile young prince, who is exiled from his kingdom of [[Gosina]] by his father and the king, [[Kelak]] for not being a powerful and worthy enough prince. This is the primary drive for Savius and Dorkel throughout the entire story.  Savius soon after being exiled, set off for a purpose, and a way to go home honorably and powerful. After visiting a lake, he meets an old man. This old man tells Savius about a powerful artifact that he is in possession of. This artifact is a scroll that grant the reader the ability to manipulate matter. The old man, named Terri, mentors Savius how to use to scroll to create weapons, fire, and structures (walls, bridges, etc. ).
